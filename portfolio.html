Midnight Spills

My photographic portfolio, Midnight Spills, captures late-night spontaneity, movement, and emotional connection through candid interactions and a moody, urban aesthetic. A consistent use of red tones, grain, and flash enhances the raw, immersive texture and visually unifies the series.

Out and About

My photographic portfolio, Out and About, is a vibrant series of images that explore moments of connection between people across different cultures and environments. The collection weaves together candid interactions, traditional Japanese and Italian scenery, and snapshots of Australian beach life to celebrate the beauty of everyday encounters and shared human experiences.

Portraits

My digital art portfolio features nine illustrated portraits of celebrities who have made a meaningful impact on their communities. Each piece highlights not just their public persona, but the causes they support and the influence they’ve had in areas such as social justice, mental health, LGBTQIA+ rights, and cultural representation.
