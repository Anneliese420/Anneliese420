Anneliese Gutwenger | About Me

Anneliese's Story

Hi, I’m Anneliese Gutwenger, a creative mind with a passion for marketing, design, and digital storytelling. As Kanye West once said, “Believe in your flyness, conquer your shyness” – a mindset I embody as I pursue my Diploma in Creative Industries and Communication at Western Sydney University. With a sharp eye for branding and audience engagement, I’m excited to make an impact in the marketing world, where creativity and strategy come together to tell meaningful stories and build lasting connections.

I’m deeply driven by self-expression and the power of storytelling. Whether I’m playing bass, designing digital art, exploring animation, or experimenting with content creation, I find joy in bringing ideas to life. My extraverted personality allows me to connect easily with others, and I thrive in collaborative spaces where ideas can bounce, evolve and take shape.

I also explore different styles through abstract art, candid photography, and creative writing. These forms fuel my curiosity and allow me to stay inspired and versatile as a designer. Travel has played a huge role in shaping my creative outlook exploring Europe and Japan opened my eyes to innovative design, bold visual culture, and dynamic storytelling, which continue to inform and inspire my creative journey.
